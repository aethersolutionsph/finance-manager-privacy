# Privacy Policy for Finance Manager

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

---

## Introduction

Welcome to **Finance Manager** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Finance Manager, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We may collect the following types of personal information:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and profile picture (when you sign in with Google)
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials and authentication tokens
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, features used, crash reports, and performance data

### 1.2 Financial Information
To provide our core services, we collect and store:

- **Transaction Data**: Income and expense records, amounts, dates, categories, and descriptions
- **Account Information**: Bank account names, balances, and transaction history
- **Investment Data**: Investment accounts, holdings, values, and performance metrics
- **Lending Records**: Loan amounts, payment schedules, interest rates, and payee information
- **Tax Information**: Tax-related transactions, categories, and calculations
- **Budget Data**: Budget categories, limits, and spending patterns

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
When you use our app, we automatically collect:

- **Log Data**: App version, access times, pages viewed, and app crashes
- **Analytics Data**: Feature usage statistics, user engagement metrics
- **Device Data**: IP address, browser type, device settings

### 1.4 Information from Third-Party Services
We integrate with the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Google Services**: For authentication (Google Sign-In) and cloud storage (Firebase)
- **Firebase**: For authentication, database, and analytics
- **AdMob**: For displaying advertisements (in free tier)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- Provide and maintain our expense tracking and financial management services
- Process and store your financial transactions
- Calculate budgets, statistics, and financial insights
- Sync data across your devices
- Enable backup and restore functionality

### 2.2 Authentication & Security
- Verify your identity and manage your account
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

### 2.3 App Improvement
- Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user behavior

### 2.4 Communication
- Send important updates about the app
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you about premium features and subscription status

### 2.5 Advertising (Free Tier Only)
- Display personalized advertisements through AdMob
- Measure ad performance and effectiveness

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- Financial data is primarily stored locally on your device
- Data is stored in encrypted SQLite databases
- Sensitive information is protected with industry-standard encryption

### 3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)
When you enable cloud sync:
- Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
- Data is stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore
- Firebase follows industry-standard security practices
- Data centers comply with SOC 2 and ISO 27001 standards

### 3.3 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication using Google OAuth 2.0
- Regular security updates and patches
- Access controls and monitoring
- Secure coding practices

### 3.4 Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
- Inactive accounts: Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 90 days
- Backups: May be retained for up to 30 days for disaster recovery

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or financial information to third parties.

### 4.2 Third-Party Service Providers
We may share limited data with trusted service providers:

- **Google Firebase**: For authentication, database, and analytics
- **Google AdMob**: For displaying ads (free tier only)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law:

- To comply with legal obligations (court orders, subpoenas)
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent fraud or security threats
- In connection with legal proceedings

### 4.4 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice in the app.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your financial data within the app
- **Export**: Download your data in CSV or PDF format
- **Modify**: Edit or delete any transaction or record
- **Delete**: Remove your account and all associated data

### 5.2 Data Portability
You can export your data at any time:
- Go to **Settings** → **Backup & Restore** → **Export Data**
- Choose format (CSV, JSON, or PDF)
- Save to your device or cloud storage

### 5.3 Account Deletion
To delete your account:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Delete Account**
2. Confirm deletion
3. All data will be permanently deleted within 90 days

### 5.4 Opt-Out Options
- **Analytics**: Disable in Settings → Privacy → Analytics
- **Personalized Ads**: Disable in Settings → Privacy → Ad Personalization
- **Cloud Sync**: Disable in Settings → Backup & Restore → Cloud Sync

### 5.5 Communication Preferences
You can opt out of:
- Marketing emails (use unsubscribe link)
- Push notifications (device settings)
- In-app promotional messages (app settings)

## 7. Children's Privacy

Finance Manager is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will delete such information from our systems.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:
- EU-US Privacy Shield compliance (where applicable)
- Standard Contractual Clauses
- Adequate security measures

## 9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

### 9.1 Right to Know
You can request:
- Categories of personal information collected
- Sources of information
- Business purposes for collection
- Categories of third parties we share with

### 9.2 Right to Delete
Request deletion of your personal information (subject to exceptions).

### 9.3 Right to Opt-Out
Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data).

### 9.4 Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at aether.solutionsph@gmail.com or use the in-app "Data Request" feature.

---

## 10. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

### 10.1 Legal Basis for Processing
- **Consent**: You explicitly agree to data processing
- **Contract**: Necessary to provide our services
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improve app functionality and security

### 10.2 Your GDPR Rights
- **Right to Access**: Obtain a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Transfer data to another service
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain processing activities
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

### 10.3 Data Protection Officer
For GDPR-related inquiries, contact: aether.solutionsph@gmail.com

### 10.4 Supervisory Authority
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 11. Cookies and Tracking

### 11.1 Cookies
Our app does not use traditional web cookies. However, we use similar technologies:

- **Local Storage**: Store app preferences and settings
- **Session Tokens**: Maintain login sessions
- **Analytics Identifiers**: Track app usage (can be disabled)

### 11.2 Do Not Track
We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals. When DNT is enabled, we disable analytics tracking.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

- Posting the new policy in the app

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
